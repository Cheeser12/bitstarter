Power to the People is a platform for political candidates to share their ideas with their (possibly) future constituents and to compare and contrast themselves with their fellow candidates. With your help, we can change the future of political elections to be citizen-centered, allowing the people to be informed of what candidates are running and what their platforms are so they can make an informed decision in the voting booth.

Benefits for Candidates

  • A social platform to connect directly with voters
  • Provides social metrics to judge how your campaign is performing
  • A non-discriminatory area to share your ideas and campaign on equal footing: everyone pays the same rate, everyone has equal access to services
  • Clearly communicate your ideas to votes and contrast yourself to other candidates by providing answers to common political questions which will be shared on your main campaign page
  • Post videos and pictures from the campaign trail
  • Full integration with other social media sites (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)

Benefits for Citizens

  • An easy way to see which candidates are running in local, state, and national elections
  • Compare and contrast candidiates by viewing their answers on common political questions
  • Use your own profile to express your support for various candidates and share with friends and family
  • Full integration with other social media sites (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)

FAQ

Will Power to the People be available for elections outside the United States?
At this time, we have no plans to support elections in countries besides the United States. However, we have not ruled out this possibility.
